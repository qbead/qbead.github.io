The Paper Booklet of Activities

The SpinWheel STEM kit can optionally contain a paper booklet, meant as a gentler introduction to some of the concepts taught by the kit. All of the content of the book is already accessible through this website, in many cases including a richer multimedia experience, however the paper medium can occasionally feel more approachable and intimate.
